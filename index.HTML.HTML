Waddle and Louisa story

Slowly and deliberately, Waddle approached the mighty dragon. A rustle in the tree of the nearby forest distracted his attention for a brief moment, a near fatal mistake for the brave knight.

The dragon lugged at him, searing waddle's armor with a rapid blast of fiery breath. waddle fell to the ground as the dragon hovered him. He quickly drew his sword and thrust it into the dragon's chest.

The dragon fell to the ground, releasing an anguised cry and seething in pain. The thrust of waddle's sword proved fatal as the dragon breathed its last breath. Now waddle was free to release Lady Louisa from her imprisonment in the dragon's lair.
